Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start my project?

+
Depending on my current workload, I can typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks. For urgent projects, I can arrange priority scheduling. Contact me to discuss your specific timeline needs.

Do you offer payment plans?

+
Yes! I offer flexible payment plans for projects over $50. Typically, I work with a deposit upfront and milestone-based payments throughout the project. We can discuss a payment structure that works for your budget.

Can we schedule a video call before starting?

+
Absolutely! I encourage a free 30-minute consultation call before any project. This helps us get to know each other, discuss your needs, and ensure we're a good fit. You can book a call using my Calendly link above.

What information should I provide when contacting you?

+
The more details, the better! Include your project goals, any specific features you need, your timeline, budget range, and any reference websites or designs you like. This helps me provide you with an accurate quote and timeline.

Do you work with international clients?

+
Yes! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with different time zones. I'm flexible with meeting times and use tools like Slack, Zoom, and email to ensure smooth communication regardless of location.

What happens after I submit the contact form?

+
I'll review your message and respond within 24 hours (usually much sooner). We'll set up a call or exchange emails to discuss your project in detail, and I'll provide you with a detailed proposal and quote.
